The Brand

Ingrid
Munt

"Creation is a feeling that transcends. This feeling makes every piece timeless, unique in life with personality."

Ingrid Munt brand was born in Barcelona in 2013, where the streets speak and artists have dreamed for hundreds of years. The architecture, the people and the bohemian style inspire her creations.

Her unique way of knitting and using fabrics consistently builds her self-expressed identity. Craftsmanship and made in Barcelona is in our DNA.

Ingrid Munt will continue to dress those free and extraordinary women around the world.
